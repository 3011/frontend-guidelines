# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Contributors and maintainers are expected to make participation respectful, constructive, and safe. Examples of positive behavior include:

- discussing ideas and evidence rather than attacking people;
- giving clear, actionable feedback;
- acknowledging different experience levels and perspectives;
- protecting private or sensitive information;
- accepting correction and repairing harm.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate disruption, sexualized conduct, publishing private information, or sustained personal attacks.

## Scope

This standard applies in repository discussions, pull requests, issues, reviews, and other spaces where someone represents the project.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through an available private repository or account contact channel. Do not disclose sensitive details in a public issue.

Maintainers may edit or remove content, reject contributions, restrict participation, or take other proportionate action. Reports will be reviewed as confidentially and fairly as practical.
